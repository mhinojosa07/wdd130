Our mission: “Delivering thrilling, safe, and unforgettable rafting adventures down every river.”

History

Founded in 1998, Whitewater Rafting Company began as a family-owned outfitter on the Colorado River. Over the years, we’ve expanded to five major rivers across the Southwest, leading over 100,000 trips and teaching thousands to conquer their first rapids. Today, we continue that legacy by combining local expertise, top-notch guides, and a passion for the water.
